How Bimmo Protects Your Data

Your privacy is our priority. We use End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) to ensure your financial data remains your own.

Store Your Assets

Bimmo as a Courier

Bimmo acts as a secure courier, encrypting your data locally before it ever leaves your device.

Secure in the Vault

Your data is stored in a digital brankas (vault) that only you can access with your private key.
