Short FAQ

Is Bimmo free to use?

Yes, Bimmo has a free plan with basic features sufficient for daily personal financial recording. We also offer a Premium plan for more complete features.

How is my data security at Bimmo?

Data security is our top priority. Bimmo uses bank-level encryption to protect all your transaction data and personal information.

Can I access Bimmo from Mobile and Laptop?

Of course! Bimmo is cloud-based, so you can access your account from any device (Android, iOS, or Web Browser) in real-time.

How to Register

  1. Access the registration page via https://app.bimmo.id/daftar.
  2. Fill in all fields completely and correctly.
  3. Check the box "I agree and accept the terms and conditions".
  4. Click the "Create an account" button to complete the registration process.

How to Login

  1. Access the login page via https://app.bimmo.id.
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Click the "Login" button.
